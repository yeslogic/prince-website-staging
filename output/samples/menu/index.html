Welcome to Prince's incredible interactive restaurant menu generator. Edit the forms below as you please and press the "Generate menu" button. The form is then sent to a server where Prince is running, turning the menu into a delicious PDF document which is returned to your browser. You can also make choices depending on the paper size in your printer and the atmosphere of your restaurant. Fonts are selected in a semi-random manner, so make sure you generate more than one menu!
